Chocolate Chip Cookies

Description

These are some of the most AMAZING cookies that you will ever bake in your life! The ingredients are very minimal and can be found in about any grocery store. It also doesn't take a long time to bake these cookies, so if your looking for a quick snack this is the perfect recipe!

Prep: 20 mins

Cook: 10 mins

Additional: 30 mins

Total: 1 hr

Servings: 24

Yield: 4 dozen

Ingredients

Now the thing you've all been waiting for. What are the ingredients for creating such a magnificent bach of chocolate chip cookies?

  • 1 Cup butter, softened
  • 1 cup white sugar
  • 1 cup packed brown sugar
  • 2 eggs
  • 2 teaspoons vanilla extract
  • 1 teaspoon baking soda
  • 2 teaspoons hot water
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 3 cups all-purpose flour
  • 2 cups semisweet chocolate chips
  • 1 cup chopped walnuts

Directions

Now that you have the ingedients in hand, it's finally time to get to baking!

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C)
  2. Cream together the butter, white sugar, and brown sugar until smooth. Beat in the eggs one at a time, then stir in the vanilla. Dissolve baking soda in hot water. Add to batter along with salt. Stir in flour, chocolate chips, and nuts. Drop by large spoonfuls onto ungreased pans.
  3. Bake for about 10 minutes in the preheated oven, or until edges are nicely browned.

And just like that you just have created a batch of freshly baked cookies in just 3 steps! Easy ain't it?
